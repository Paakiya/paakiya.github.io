02 / APPROACH

How I work

Every automation I ship follows roughly the same disciplined loop — adapted from UiPath's REFramework, but really it's just how I think about any process before touching code.

01

Discover

Map the manual process end-to-end, flag exceptions and edge cases, and confirm where automation creates real leverage.

02

Design

Architect the workflow on REFramework — define queues, transaction items, and the exception-handling strategy before writing a line of code.

03

Build

Develop in UiPath Studio with reusable components, wiring in SAP, Excel, SharePoint, Outlook, or internal portals as the process requires.

04

Test & Harden

Validate against real edge cases, build in retries, and lock down credentials and assets through Orchestrator.

05

Deploy & Monitor

Ship to Orchestrator, watch the first live runs closely, and stay hands-on with uptime and exceptions after go-live.
